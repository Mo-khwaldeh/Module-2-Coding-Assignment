Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a beloved espresso-based hot coffee drink made with layering of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam on top. The taste of cappuccinos can be described as creamy, smooth, and balanced. The combination of flavors and textures creates a delightful and comforting experience for many coffee enthusiasts.

Espresso

Espresso coffee is a dark, concentrated Italian- made coffee. It consists of a small amount of boiling water with ground coffee beans. It is served in a small cup with a saucer, similar in shape to a Turkish coffee cup , but it is often thick and white in color, and can be transparent.

Latte

The latte is known for being one of the more simple and easier coffees to brew from home, with plenty of room for customization. A true latte’s measurements consist of ⅓ espresso and ⅔ steamed milk, with a small layer of frothy milk foam to complete the drink’s classic look.
